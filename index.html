“Great food. Great people. Remarkable university.
The students have kept me here all this time.”

Jean Couto

Supervisor of Dining Operations

Jean went to school at Johnson & Wales and Rivier College and came to Brown right after graduating. She has been here ever since. She wakes up at 4am every day to get to Brown and start work. In regard to her daily routine and the people she works with she says, “every morning I enjoy getting up. We all care about each other- it’s like a second family.”

“The staff has such diverse backgrounds; it makes them what they are, and I am so impressed by everyone’s effort. Everybody works so cohesively and closely. It becomes like a second family.”

Bob Machado

Assistant Director for Residential Dining

Bobby started working in contract health care before working here, and so he appreciates the longevity of the workers at Brown. “I would encourage students to take tours of the Ratty to learn about what goes on behind-the-scenes, to see the quality and freshness of the food. We have our own baker shop and butcher shop, which is pretty unique among colleges. People don’t realize it, but Brown has such an impressive sustainability initiative and sources quality ingredients from local farmers.”

“You are in a good place, and there are people
thinking of you everyday. When you leave home,
this is your second home, and we are working
for you and caring about you. It's our job.”

Kamel

Cook's Helper

Kamel moved from France 15 years ago after going to a French culinary school. He worked in other restaurants before coming to Brown. At Brown, he says “There’s something different everyday. It’s like a mass-scale restaurant. I’ve work at everything in the kitchen, and work on anything that is needed. I like the challenge.”

Process

So much happens behind the scenes at the Ratty - both before food gets to your plate, and after. This is a sneak peek into two processes that fly under the radar.

Preparation of Food

The food at the Ratty is prepared with love. From the purchasing of prime ingredients to the time it hits your plate, everyone at the Ratty has put care into your food.

Spread the Love

As students and members of the Brown community, we must recognize and reciprocate the love and affection the Ratty staff have for us. So, next time you're at the Ratty, try some of these things to show them your appreciation.

  • Make their lives easier
  • Say hello to the people who serve you food
  • Strike up a conversation while you're in line
  • Say Thank You
